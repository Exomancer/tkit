tkit

(T)opology Kit

Adds a few operators for selection manipulation in edge mode, most of which are more easily understood by experience than by description.

A good medium for observation and testing is a subdivided mesh, IN EDGE MODE, with a few stray edges selected.

Quote: Deselect edges at the "edge" of the selection.

Shift + Quote: Deselect edges on the "inside" of the selection.

Left Bracket: Un-Extend the edge selection by deselecting the "tips".

Right Bracket: Extend the edge selection.

Backslash: Select edges that are topologically "perpendicular" to the selected edges.

Shift+Backslash: A variation of the preceding.

Control + Shift + Backslash: Another Variation.

Control + Shift + Alt + Backslash: Another Variation.

Control + Alt + Shift + End: Selects edges at the "tips".
